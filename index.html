About ToonMedia
We TMG , an Indo-Japanese Venture and a leading Consulting Company with 20 Years of industry focused on providing optimum and Timely innovative IT Solutions to organizations, & Connecting Leading Talents [ both Bilingual & Non-Bilingual ] with Leading Companies in India, Japan & APAC.

ToonMedia was founded by Mr. B G Sreedhara, a young entrepreneur, who brings to his clients over seven years of IT - ITES industry experience and has been instrumental in providing innovative, cost effective, quality solutions, through his unique blend of entrepreneurial and business skills. More than the spirit, it is the quality of work, the people — Their Character, Philosophy, Possession & Style — that most distinguishes ToonMedia from any other firm in this line of business.
